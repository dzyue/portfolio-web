EcoGrow

APP Design UI/UX

It is an APP aims to motivate the habit of daily recycling while making the process easy and fun by adopting the gamification strategy. It shows the user the visualized information of recycling , and encourages the behaviour of continuous recycling through a digital planting game.


When logging in, user will be directed to the dashboard/homepage, offering the options of using the recycling code, redeem code, booking a pick-up service for oversize recycling, and getting recycling bags.


Recycling guide is embedded inside the APP. It helps user build up the recycling knowledge by taking and reviewing related lessons on the APP, or scanning product’s barcode to learn about its recyclability.


When recycling properly, the user will be rewarded the “Eco Points”, which can be used for purchasing seeds and flower pots to start the planting game. User can check the planting status anytime in their “digital garden”, which also reveals the statistics of their recycling history.

Social connection is a part of the design, which makes it more fun when you can “steal” seeds and energy from your friends!
