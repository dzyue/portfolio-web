PARKER: APP UX Design

Car parking has always been a wicked problem in China due to the increasing numbers of private cars and relatively limited space. A new intelligent parking service system may be a solution to improve the parking service quality.

After competitor study and the previous analysis, I started to think about a better solution. The new service system will provide the following functions:

i. Help user to find a parking lot and know its vacancy;

ii. Help user to find the parked car in the parking lot;

iii. Quickly drive away from the parking lot by pre-paying or paying later on the APP;

iv. Utilize the unused parking space by renting to others (in the semi-public parking lot.)

The APP has four major sections: search and book for a parking lot (i.e. Search), check the booked parking lot (i.e. Booked), rent unused parking space to others (i.e. Share), and view personal information(i.e. Account).


Under the “Search” section, user can type in the location, or swipe up the window to get a detailed search: searching by category, by distance, by price, etc.




After getting car parked within the parking lot, user can use the APP as a memo to mark down the parking code. When returning back to the parking lot, user can reopen the APP to check the code and get a navigation to the parked space.


When choosing “Finish the parking”, the system will automatically calculate the parking time and will ask user to make an extra payment if necessary.


“Share” section is designed for those who owned a semi-public parking space and want to rent to others when it is unused (usually during the day time). By clicking to add a shared space, user can find his/her owned parking space location, and enter the space information such as its floor level, parking code, and the related contract document.
